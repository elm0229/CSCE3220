Fish can make beautiful pets. They come in so many colors and some can even glow! They aren't by any means low maintenance and depending on the fish can be quite difficult to manage. Fish are generally kept in diverse group because they must live in a well kept environment. When you decide to own a fish, you're also deciding to build and maintain a small world! The pet is less the fish and more the aquarium. Fish are also known for being natural prey to birds. Learn more about fish here.
