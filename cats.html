Cats are well known for being very independent yet affectionate pets. They are very adept at taking care of their own needs like grooming. Cats can also be outdoor pets which adds to their independent nature. They have a number of breeds that can be identified by the length and color of their fur. Cats have predator instincts and enjoy playing with small toys that imitate mice or bugs but don't be surprised if they come home with a real one! Cats are also known for being natural enemies to fish. Learn more about cats here.
