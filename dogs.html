Dogs are probably the most popular pet in the world. They aren't called "Man's best friend" for nothing. Dogs can come in a variety of sizes from the smallest Chihuahua to the largest Great Dane. Dogs are very intelligent and can even perform life-saving tasks as service animals. They are extremely loyal and playful creatures and make great pets for families. Dogs are also known for being natural enemies to cats. Learn more about dogs here.
